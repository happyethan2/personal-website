Keto has made a profound impact on my life in ways I didn't expect. My motivations were initially data-driven, which if you know me is no surprise. SiBio offers the market's only continuous ketone monitor which I heard of in March this year from a good friend. I often feel like I wouldnt've stuck to it if not for a graph on my phone that continually provides me with feedback. Nonetheless I did, and I'm still going months later even though I thought I'd have stopped by now.

Aside from data, the big motivator was what I heard about cognitive health. Keto's incredible theraputic potential in treating psychiatric disorders captivated my attention.
