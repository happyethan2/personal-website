ingredients:
  - "1000g chicken thigh, bitesized"
  - "100 g ghee (or butter)"
  - "1 cup (250mL) thickened cream"
  - "2 tbsp coconut oil"
  - "4 cloves garlic, minced"
  - "1 tbsp fresh ginger (grated)"
  - "2 tbsp tomato paste"
  - "1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes"
  - "2 tsp garam masala"
  - "1.5 tsp ground cumin"
  - "1 tsp ground coriander"
  - "1 tsp smoked paprika"
  - "0.5 tsp tumeric"
  - "0.5 tsp chilli powder (optional)"
  - "Salt & Pepper (to taste)"
  - "Fresh coriander leaves"
  - "50g full-fat greek yoghurt"
  - "1 tbsp lemon juice"

method:
  - "Dice chicken thighs into bite-sized chunks"
  - "Chop onion finely"
  - "Mince garlic and grate ginger"
  - "Prepare spices by mixing them together into an appropriate container"
  - "Heat 2 tbsp ghee (or butter) in a large enough pan"
  - "Add chicken pieces (in batches to avoid water build-up), season lightly with salt/pepper and sear until browned (don't fully cook through)"
  - "Remove chicken and set aside"
  - "In the same pan, add another 2-3 tbsp ghee (or butter)"
  - "Add onion and cook until soft and golden (4-8 mins)"
  - "Add garlic + ginger and stir for 1 min until fragrant"
  - "Stir in the spice mix to bloom and cook for 30-60 sec until aromatic"
  - "Add tomato paste and cook for 1-2 mins to deepen flavour"
  - "Add crushed tomatoes and stir + simmer for 5-10 mins or until sauce thickens slightly"
  - "Stir in cup of heavy cream"
  - "Add seared chicken back into pan"
  - "Simmer gently until chicken is cooked through at sauce is smooth and thickened (approx 10-15 min)"
  - "Stir in the remaining ghee (or butter) + lemon juice right at the end"
  - "Taste and adjust salt/pepper"
  - "Garnish with chopped coriander and greek yoghurt (50g/serve)"
