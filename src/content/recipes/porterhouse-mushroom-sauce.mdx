ingredients:
  - "300–350 g porterhouse steak, room temp"
  - "200 g mushrooms, sliced"
  - "20 g butter (sear) + 20 g butter (sauce)"
  - "80 ml thickened cream (use 60 ml if you prefer lighter)"
  - "1 small shallot, minced"
  - "1 clove garlic, minced"
  - "1 tsp Dijon mustard (optional)"
  - "Salt & pepper"

method:
  - "Pat steak dry and salt generously. Sear in hot pan with butter 2–3 min/side; baste; rest 5 min."
  - "Same pan: sauté shallot & mushrooms until browned."
  - "Add garlic 30s, deglaze with a splash of water/stock; scrape fond."
  - "Stir in cream and remaining butter; simmer to coat. Season; add mustard if using."
  - "Slice steak; spoon sauce over. Serve."
